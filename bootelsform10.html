Study English in Canada

Learn English in Canada

English proficiency can open doors to so many opportunities. Learning and developing strong English language skills is the key to reaching your educational, career, and life goals. With prestigious universities and colleges; safe and clean cities and friendly, welcoming communities, it is no surprise that Canada is a top destination for international students.

Study at Toronto, Ontario

ELS/Toronto is situated in Canada’s largest city, in the midst of the country’s commercial and cultural center. This dynamic city Center is within walking distance of many attractions, including museums and galleries, sports venues, and theaters. Venture outside of downtown to see the natural beauty of the Toronto Islands. Trips to Niagara Falls, and tours of Ottawa, Montreal, Quebec, New York City, Chicago, and Boston are among the most popular weekend activities.

Study at Vancouver, British Columbia

ELS/Vancouver is conveniently located in the heart of downtown Vancouver, a city of rare natural beauty and diverse cultures considered to be the jewel of Canada’s West Coast. Vancouver is consistently ranked among the most livable cities in the world, and enjoys comfortable temperatures all year round. Visits to Whistler, Victoria and Seattle as well as multiday tours of the Rocky Mountains are popular weekend student activities.

English for Academic Purposes

English for Academic Purposes (EAP)

The English for Academic Purposes Program is designed for international students who want to attend one of the colleges or universities in Canada, including students who wish to apply for conditional admission to an ELS partner institution.

This intensive ELS English for Academic Purposes (EAP) program can help prepare you for success in an academic setting. Morning classes are dedicated to Structure and Speaking Practice or Language Studies, Vocabulary Enrichment (in levels 101-109) or Skills Enhancement Classes (in Masters levels), and a lesson in the Language Technology Center. Afternoon classes are dedicated to Reading and Writing.
