Learn English in India

India is a welcoming and educational atmosphere for students who are interested in improving their English skills. When you choose India as your study destination, you will enjoy the same high-quality English programs as you would experience in other parts of the world, combined with India’s affordable cost of living. In addition, India’s visa and enrollment policies are straightforward and welcoming to international students from all over the world.

Choose Chennai

Studying in India offers you a strong, high-quality educational system with English-medium universities, high-achieving alumni, and proven leadership in STEM fields. In addition, studying in India will allow you to enjoy the country’s vibrant culture and rich history. Spend your free time sightseeing, enjoying local cuisine and cultural activities, and living comfortably within your means due to India’s affordable cost of living. Become a part of India’s proud 50-year history of supporting and enriching the lives of international students from all over the world!

Study at SRM University

SRM University, home to ELS/Chennai, is one of the highest-ranked universities in India, and offers a variety of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral degree programs. Students are drawn to the diverse and welcoming campus of SRM University for its reputable programs, state-of-the-art facilities, cutting-edge research, diverse faculty, and invaluable global networking opportunities. Scholarships are available for international undergraduate students, adding even more value to the already-affordable tuition costs.

SRM students travel around campus either on foot or by bicycle. The university operates buses to transport students into Chennai city and to other key points, and there are also public bus and rail stops not far from campus. SRM is 40 kilometers from the center of Chennai and 20 kilometers from Chennai Airport, making the campus easily accessible by road, rail, and air.
